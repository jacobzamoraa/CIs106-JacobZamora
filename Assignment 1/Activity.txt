How to make a turket sandwhich
1. Start with two pieces of white bread.
2. while both breads are spread open with nothing on them.
Put the mayo on on both pieces of the bread
3. While both pieces of bread have mayo on them.
choose one side to add the turkey onto.
4. place the mayo side of the second piece of bread onto the top of the first bread with the turkey.
5. place sandwhich on plate and cut crust off.
